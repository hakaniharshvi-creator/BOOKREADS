About BookRead

The world's largest community for readers.

Our Story

BookRead was founded in 2007 with a simple mission: help people find and share the books they love. What began as a small cataloguing tool has grown into the world's most vibrant reading community, connecting millions of readers, authors, and book clubs across the globe.

We believe that books have the power to transform lives, spark conversation, and bridge cultures. Our platform gives every reader a personal library, a social feed, and access to the collective wisdom of 90 million book lovers.

From debut novels to classic literature, from self-help to speculative fiction, BookRead is your home for every book you've ever loved — and every one you've yet to discover.
